Introduction

Welcome to Call Query Assistant, an AI assistant developed by ChicoAI for querying call transcripts. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use this app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or log any data from your interactions with the app. Specifically:

  • We do not store your inputs (queries).
  • We do not collect metadata (e.g., timestamps, session IDs).
  • The app only temporarily accesses the content of the Google Doc to process your query and returns the relevant information.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to fulfill your query by retrieving and summarizing relevant information from the document. No data is stored or retained after the query is processed.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. The app relies solely on the Google Doc API to access the document content, which is processed securely in real-time.

Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that your queries and document access are handled securely. All interactions with the Google Doc API are encrypted via HTTPS.

However, no system is entirely secure, and we encourage you to avoid submitting sensitive or confidential information through the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to use the app without concern about data storage or misuse, as no data is retained. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
